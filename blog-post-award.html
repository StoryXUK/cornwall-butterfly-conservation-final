Butterflies of Cornwall: Atlas for the Twenty-first Century

Soaring over the challenges of the last eighteen months, we are pleased to celebrate the publication in June 2021 of Butterflies of Cornwall: Atlas for the Twenty-first Century. This is Cornwall Butterfly Conservation’s lavishly illustrated book on the County’s butterflies: their population trends and distribution from Bude to Land’s End and the Isles of Scilly, with much else besides.

We are absolutely thrilled to announce that the Butterflies of Cornwall, has won the prestigious Holyer an Gof Publisher’s Award in the category of ‘Science and Environment’.
